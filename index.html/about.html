About Me

Born in Kerala and raised in Chennai, I am an ambitious individual with a deep passion for both technology and creativity. I completed my 10th grade at St. John's Matriculation Higher Secondary School with a 91% score, followed by achieving 93% in my 12th grade at Jaigopal Garodia Higher Secondary School. Throughout my school years, I won numerous medals in athletics at both school and interschool levels, along with awards for my creative endeavors.

Art has always been a significant part of my life, leading to a personal collection of over 500 hand-drawn images. Though I shifted from being extroverted to more introverted at the age of 15, I still enjoy meeting new people and have connected with individuals from over 20 countries through phone conversations.

Currently, I am pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, focusing on web development and design. In addition, I am working on building my own agency, Criativo, which provides creative and tech services to clients. My goal is to scale the agency further by assembling a strong team.

As an Indian, I am driven by an unyielding desire to live an extraordinary life, striving to be among the top 1% of achievers. My ambitions are fueled by inspirations such as Mark Zuckerberg, Elon Musk, and Bill Gates, who have all transformed their industries through innovation. I am committed to continuous learning and personal growth, not only in business but also in life. My routine includes regular visits to the gym, which helps me maintain focus and discipline.

In addition to my professional pursuits, I am passionate about traveling and dream of exploring as many countries as possible, combining my love for creativity, technology, and global culture.
